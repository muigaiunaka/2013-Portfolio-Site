WEB DESIGN

Process: muigaiunaka.com

The goal of creating this website was to not only showcase works that Muigai has designed and/or developed but to convey his personality, show the thinking behind his design decisions and to display the process of completing his work.

Initially, as shown in the wireframe below, Muigai had hoped to develop a parallax scrolling, single page site that would show his web design, print design and photography. After receiving critiques from Northeastern University's student-led design studio, Scout, Muigai made various changes to the design. Muigai added Web Design and Print Design in the navigation bar to ensure clear navigation for the user. Muigai kept to a minimal color palette, using the colors in his background image that the user sees upon reaching the site. Muigai chose PT Sans as his lead typeface for its on screen readability.

Currently, Muigai is working on the responsiveness of the portfolio site on mobile and tablet applications as well as the loading speed of the site. Muigai is also working on a better way to display his photographic work as well as a more appropriate footer.
