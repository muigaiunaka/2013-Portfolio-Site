Graphic Design II

Fall 2014

For the class, Graphic Design 2, each student was tasked with designing a Public Social Cause campaign for a social cause of their choice. The root social cause chosen was "secondhand-smoke in the presence of children", with a target audience of parents and/or caregivers. The campaign is entitled 'When The Smoke Clears' and the mission of the campaign is to inform parents and/or caregivers who smoke in the presence of children of the perilous effects of secondhand smoke on children. Each piece shown to the left is a part of a series of three poster designs. The typograpic cigarette was designed using Adobe InDesign. The color palette included: #CD9653, #FFFFFF and #000000 because the colors match those of a cigarette. The typeface used was Agency FB, it was chosen for it's clear readability, square form and weight helps the type become a focal point.
